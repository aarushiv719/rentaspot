Rent-a Spot




Rent-a-Spot is a service that allows land and homeowners to list open spaces on their property, and users/clients can rent these spaces to park their vehicles for daily or monthly prices. Both property owners and users/clients will create accounts and each type of account will have different features. Daily and monthly fees will be determined by the owner of the property and a description of the type of surface, over-head covering (if there is one available), and the dimensions of their parking space will be provided to the user/client. An aerial view/image of the spot at the location that is procured from Google Maps will also be shown with the listing (if a garage space is being offered, a picture of the space will be provided by the property owner instead of the aerial image). Users/clients will provide the make and model of their vehicle along with a license plate number (provided at the time of the transaction). If a user/client has multiple vehicles that need to be parked, this will be completed through multiple transactions (one for each vehicle). Both types of accounts will also have profiles and phone numbers to identify and contact each other. The property owner profiles will always be available, but the client profile will only be made visible to the property owner after the payment has been given. All transactions between property owners and users will happen in-person.
